# Prompt for AI Presentation Tool

Copy and paste the prompt below into ChatGPT, Gamma.app, Beautiful.ai, or any AI presentation tool.

---

## THE PROMPT

```
Create a professional PowerPoint presentation (10-12 slides) for a sales pitch to a Nigerian printing company. The presentation introduces an AI Agent System built by Evawero Digital Solutions that automates their marketing, sales, and project management.

BRAND COLOURS:
- Primary green: #1D9E75
- Dark green: #0F6E56
- White: #FFFFFF
- Dark text: #111111
- Light background: #F5F5F5

COMPANY: Evawero Digital Solutions
LOGO TEXT: "ED" (green square with white text)
WEBSITE: evawerodigital.com

TARGET AUDIENCE: Owner/manager of a mid-size printing company in Lagos, Nigeria. Not technical. Cares about getting more clients, saving time, and looking professional.

---

SLIDE 1 — TITLE
Title: "Your Printing Business, Running Smarter"
Subtitle: "AI Agents That Find Clients, Create Content, and Keep You in Control"
Footer: Evawero Digital Solutions | evawerodigital.com

SLIDE 2 — THE PROBLEM
Title: "Sound Familiar?"
3-4 bullet points with icons:
- You rely on word-of-mouth and WhatsApp to find new clients
- Your social media is inconsistent — you post when you remember
- You track jobs in notebooks or WhatsApp groups
- You're too busy printing to chase new business
Tone: empathetic, not condescending

SLIDE 3 — THE SOLUTION
Title: "What If Your Business Had a Team That Never Sleeps?"
Short paragraph: "We build custom AI agents that handle your marketing, find you new clients, manage your projects, and report back to you every morning — all on autopilot."
Visual: 4 icons representing the 4 agents in a row

SLIDE 4 — MARKETING AGENT
Title: "Your Marketing Agent"
Subtitle: "Creates branded content while you focus on production"
Bullet points:
- Writes blog posts about printing tips, trends, and your services
- Creates Instagram graphics (branded quote cards, tip lists, announcements)
- Plans a content calendar — posts scheduled across the week
- All content uses your brand colours, voice, and messaging
- Everything saved as drafts — you review before anything goes live
Visual: screenshot/mockup of the Marketing tab from the demo dashboard

SLIDE 5 — SALES AGENT
Title: "Your Sales Agent"
Subtitle: "Finds qualified leads and drafts personalised outreach"
Bullet points:
- Searches the web for businesses that need printing (hotels, schools, event planners, churches, corporates)
- Adds prospects to your CRM with company details and notes
- Drafts personalised cold emails — ready for you to review and send
- Tracks the pipeline: identified → contacted → replied → booked
- Checks for replies and drafts follow-ups automatically
Visual: screenshot/mockup of the Sales tab showing lead cards and email drafts

SLIDE 6 — PROJECT MANAGEMENT AGENT
Title: "Your Project Manager"
Subtitle: "Breaks down client jobs into clear, trackable tasks"
Bullet points:
- When you win a new job, the agent creates a project plan automatically
- Breaks the job into steps (design, proofing, print run, delivery)
- Drafts a professional kickoff email to the client
- Tracks progress — you see what's done and what's next
- No more forgetting steps or losing track of jobs
Visual: screenshot/mockup of the Projects tab showing task checklist

SLIDE 7 — MANAGER AGENT
Title: "Your Daily Briefing"
Subtitle: "Everything you need to know, in one email every morning"
Bullet points:
- Sends you a digest email: what happened, what needs your attention
- Shows your lead pipeline, active projects, and content created
- Alerts you when something is urgent (a lead replied, a deadline is close)
- You start your day knowing exactly what to focus on
Visual: screenshot/mockup of the Manager tab showing the digest

SLIDE 8 — LIVE DEMO
Title: "See It in Action"
Subtitle: "We built a demo dashboard for a fictional printing company — PrintHub Lagos"
Describe the 5 tabs briefly:
- Overview: Stats at a glance — leads, content, projects, alerts
- Sales: Full pipeline with lead cards and draft emails
- Marketing: Content calendar and created posts with graphics
- Projects: Job tracking with task checklists and kickoff emails
- Manager: Daily digest with what needs your attention
Footer: "Visit the live demo: evawerodigital.com/demo/printhub"

SLIDE 9 — WHAT'S CONFIGURABLE
Title: "Built Around Your Business"
Two-column layout:
Left column - "We customise for you":
- Your brand identity (colours, logo, voice, messaging)
- Your target markets and cities
- Your services and specialties
- Your ideal client profile
- Your run schedule (daily, 3x/week, weekly)
Right column - "You control":
- Review and approve all content before publishing
- Review and send all emails before they go out
- Update lead status as conversations progress
- Set project priorities and deadlines

SLIDE 10 — WHAT YOU DO vs WHAT THE AGENTS DO
Title: "You Stay in Control"
Two-column comparison:
Left - "The Agents Handle":
- Finding new leads every week
- Writing blog posts and social content
- Drafting outreach emails
- Breaking down projects into tasks
- Sending you a daily briefing
Right - "You Handle":
- Reviewing and approving content
- Sending emails (agents only draft)
- Talking to clients
- Delivering the print jobs
- Making business decisions

SLIDE 11 — HOW WE GET STARTED
Title: "Getting Started Is Simple"
4 steps:
1. Free consultation — we learn about your business, brand, and goals
2. We build your agent team — customised to your industry and market
3. Test run — you review the output and we fine-tune
4. Go live — agents run on schedule, you review and approve
CTA: "Book a free consultation"

SLIDE 12 — CLOSING
Title: "Ready to Run Smarter?"
Subtitle: "Let AI handle the busywork while you focus on what you do best — printing."
Contact: info@evawerodigital.com
Website: evawerodigital.com
CTA button: "Book a Free Consultation — evawerodigital.com/contact"
```

## TIPS FOR BEST RESULTS

- **Gamma.app** tends to produce the best-looking slides from a prompt — paste the full prompt above
- **ChatGPT + Code Interpreter** can generate actual .pptx files if you ask it to
- **Beautiful.ai** works well for clean, modern layouts
- After generating, manually add screenshots from the demo dashboard (evawerodigital.com/demo/printhub) to slides 4-7
- Replace any stock photos with screenshots of the actual demo tabs
